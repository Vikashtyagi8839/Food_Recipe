Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon methi seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice and urad dal, and soak them in water for 4-5 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the mixture into a smooth batter.
  3. Add methi seeds and salt to the batter, and mix well.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, and pour a ladle of the batter onto it.
  5. Cook the dosa for 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to curl.
  6. Flip the dosa and cook for another minute.
  7. Serve hot with sambar and chutney.
